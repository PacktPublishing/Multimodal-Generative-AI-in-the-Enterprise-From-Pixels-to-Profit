# Global Styleguide

## Brand Guidelines
- **Primary Colors:** #FF5733 (Vibrant Sunrise), #f3d6a1 (Boring Beige), #F4F4F9 (Cloud White).
- **Mood/Tone:** Modern, energetic, premium, forward-thinking, and slightly playful.
- **Inclusions:** Cozy settings, wellbeing, clear typography.
- **Exclusions:** No photorealistic people, no cluttered backgrounds, no cheap or spammy text.

## Modality-Specific Instructions

### Text (Copywriting & Strategy)
- Keep sentences short, punchy, and action-oriented.
- Emphasize the product's innovative aspects.
- Avoid prohibited terms: "cheap", "guaranteed", "miracle", "overnight".

### Images (Visuals)
- Focus on product features, abstract representations of AI, or sleek environments.
- Lighting should be dramatic, with high contrast.
- Absolutely no human figures or human hands.

### Video
- Smooth, sweeping camera movements (e.g., slow pans, zoom-ins).
- Transitions should be seamless.
- Maintain the primary color palette throughout the sequence.
- Adhere strictly to the "NO HUMANS" exclusion.
